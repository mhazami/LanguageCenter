How to Use the Folders Tool

ESL Library's folders tool is a fantastic option for organizing the lessons you would like to save for future use. With all the materials on ESL Library, saving lessons to your own custom folders will help you plan and save time.

Step-by-Step Instructions

You can also follow the steps below to locate and use the folders tool.

Step 1

Log in to your account here: Log In

Step 2

Once you have successfully logged in, go to the Lessons page (see below).

Step 3

After landing on our Lessons page, you can start browsing the lesson sections to find one that is suitable for your class. Click on any of the lesson sections (e.g., Everyday Dialogues).

Step 4

Once you're inside a lesson section, you will notice that each lesson has a small black folder icon beneath it. Clicking the icon allows you to save that lesson to a particular folder. If the icon is already filled out in blue, then that lesson has already been saved into a folder.

Step 5

Clicking the folder icon will bring up a box with two tabs. Choose "Select Folder" to save the lesson to an existing folder(s) or choose "Create Folder" to start a new folder. If you do not have any existing folders, the only option will be to create a new folder by entering a folder name and optional description. Click the "Save" button to complete either action.

Step 6

If you click on a particular lesson, you can also save it to a folder(s) from any lesson preview page by clicking the folder icon on the top right.

Step 7

After clicking the folder icon, choose the existing folder you would like to save the lesson to (or create a new folder). Click the "Save" button to complete the action.

Step 8

To access your folders, click the My Folders tab in the navigation bar on the top of any page.

Step 9

On your Lesson Folders page, you can click the folder title to view and print the lessons inside the folder. You can also add or delete any folders or edit the name and description of your folders on this page.

We hope the folders tool helps you save time and stay organized! Please reach out to us if you have any questions or suggestions on how to use the folders.
